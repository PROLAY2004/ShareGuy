About ShareGuy

Welcome to ShareGuy, a simple and secure file-sharing platform where you can upload and share files without the need for login or registration.

How It Works

ShareGuy allows you to upload files and share them effortlessly. Here's how it works:

  • Upload Your File: Simply upload your file to our platform.
  • QR Code: After uploading, a QR code will be generated. Scan it with another device, and the file will start downloading.
  • Email Feature: Enter your email address, and the uploaded file will be sent directly to your inbox.
  • Unique Code: A unique code will be generated. Share this code with the recipient, and they can use it to download the file from the "Receive" section.

Why Choose ShareGuy?

  • No Login Required: You don't need to create an account or log in to use ShareGuy.
  • Privacy: We do not store any user information, ensuring your privacy.
  • Global Access: Share files from anywhere in the world with ease.
  • Safe File Transfer: Files are automatically cleared from our server after 5 minutes, ensuring secure and temporary storage.

Limitations

  • Maximum file size: 20MB.
  • Files are valid for only 5 minutes on our server.
  • No peer-to-peer connection.
  • Once an email is sent, the session is over, even if the email address is incorrect. There is no option for correction.
